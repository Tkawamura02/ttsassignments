About Me

General Information

Who am I?
My name is Tiffany Kawamura and I am a Biomedical Science major student. I take Applied Computing as my minor and tech programs with my free time. I am currently doing photosynthesis research at ASU (Arizona State University) and attending the TTS program learning how to FullStack Develop. I also have an internship as a UI/UX developer virtually.

What am I passionate about?
I love to be around animals and be supportive to the people that I love. Being there for people who are unable to do so for themselves brings me immense sense of purpose that nothing else can bring. However, I have also recently found coding to be one of the things that I love to do for myself because I understand that working for others (giving them my time and having them as the source of my own happiness) is not the best thing to do for personal health.

What am I currently doing?
I am currently a Call Support Representative at Blue Cross Blue Shield of Arizona. I got there because I applied through ASU's Education at Work Program. I enjoy my job there but I know that it is not my goal to stay there forever. I am also taking tech programs during my free time to improve the outlook for my future (career-wise).
